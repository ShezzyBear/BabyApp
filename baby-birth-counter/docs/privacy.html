Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

Baby Birth Counter ("the App") is developed by Shez Bhagwandin. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from those providers.

Birth records: Each entry you log includes date and time, biological sex, delivery method, and optional notes you provide.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the App's functionality — storing and displaying your birth records. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose beyond operating the App.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). Firebase's privacy practices are described at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Third-Party Sign-In

Sign-in is handled by Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In. We receive only basic profile information (name and email) from these providers. Their respective privacy policies govern how they handle your credentials.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your records are retained as long as your account exists. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at shezzywezzy@gmail.com and we will process the request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by healthcare professionals and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
